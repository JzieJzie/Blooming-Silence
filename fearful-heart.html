Fearful Heart's Enemy

Time won’t stop to watch you doubt, It walks away while you figure things out. You stall, you hope, you think it through But time moves on without a cue.

You keep your words, afraid to fall, In fear they won’t mean much at all. So close your lips, miss your chance While love walks by without a glance.

You tell yourself, maybe next day, But somehow, that gets swept away. And what you feel just fades to ache A silent heart, a slow heartbreak.

They laugh, they leave, they never knew The things you never said were true. Not lost in war, or some great fight Just gone because you froze that night.

The ones who love, they speak, they try. They risk the no, they brave goodbye. But those who wait for perfect signs Are left behind by steady time.

So say it now. Don’t waste the fire. The heart grows cold with each desire That’s kept too long, or tucked away And never lives to see the day.
